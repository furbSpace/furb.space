who am i

My name is Zachary Furbush. I am currently an undergraduate student majoring in mathematics (previously aerospace engineering for 3 years). I am specifically interested in the computation of differential-based physics as it pertains to various simulations and aerospace applications, like guidance and control systems. I love to learn constantly, and always attempt to learn more. I also believe in a balance of art and science in life. Art is an expression of someone or something, and an important part of humanity. I have always loved to create. From as early as I can remember, I have always been making stuff. I like to approach things the hard way first to really understand what I am doing and more importantly why. This approach to everything in life is the reason I am who I am. I am a work in progress, and I put work into my progress.
